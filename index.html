MassRemoveArcs

MassRemoveArcs is a small plugin I developed for the Beat Saber map editor ChroMapper.
This simple plugin allowed users to quickly remove any arcs they may have made that they want to get rid off, since this isn't built in behaviour.
While a small project, this project allowed me to learn how to maintain a product through regular updates.
