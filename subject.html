Subject #48

Subject information:

Subject #48 is a being that first came to our attention in 1898, we do not know when it was made or how it was made, but it is believed it is older than the universe itself. It was found under the ownership of a famous actor, who used it for his own gain. We do not know the limits of its power, but it includes creating and destroying matter at will, which was previously thought impossible. Subject #48 works kind of like a genie, granting anything a person wants.

Why is it dangerous?

As mentioned before, we do not know the limits of its power. We do know, however, that a cult has started looking and worshipping the subject. Subject #48 is currently under extreme security measures, because we do not know what this cult would do with it. If it falls into the wrong hands, it could very well threaten our entire existence.
